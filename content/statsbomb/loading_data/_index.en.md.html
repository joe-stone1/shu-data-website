Statsbomb have put the specification documents for StatsBomb Data. These are available to view or download and define key apsects to the data set.

These documents include:

Open Data Competitions.pdf- Covers the objects contained within the competitions information

Open Data Matches v3.0.0.pdf - Describes the match info download

Open Data Lineups v2.0.0.pdf - Describes the structure of the lineup info
